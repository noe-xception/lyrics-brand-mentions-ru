About the Project

This dashboard presents data from a corpus study tracking brand name mentions in Russian chart music. The project analyzes brand representation and frequency over time.

  • Corpus Collection: The database was compiled from chart archives, including TopHit, and music websites.
  • AI Processing: Large Language Models (LLMs) matched free-text artist and song names with lyrics from APIs, identified brand mentions, resolved slang, and mapped aliases.
  • Data Enrichment: Extracted entities were enriched with metadata, such as brand categories and corporate affiliations.
  • Open Data Licensing: The annotated corpus and aggregated dataset are made available for academic research under a Creative Commons (CC BY-NC 4.0) license.
